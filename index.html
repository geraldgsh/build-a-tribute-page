Ralph .C Smedley

Founder of Toastmasters International

Here's a time line of Dr. Ralph C. Smedley's life:

  • 1878 - Born in Waverly, Illinois, United States
  • 1903 - Started work at YMCA
  • 1910 - Appointed as General Secretary at YMCA in at Rock Island, Illinois
  • 1919 - Accepted the post of YMCA Secretary at San Jose, California
  • 1924 - First Toastmasters meeting at YMCA
  • 1928 - Ralph obtained copyrights on his publications and copyrighted the name Toastmasters International
  • 1930 - 30 clubs were formed
  • 1932 - Toastmasters International was incorporated as a California Non-profit corporation.
  • 1941 - Resigned as YMCA Secretary
  • 1950 - Smedley wrote Beyond Basic Training
  • 1956 - Toastmasters gave Smedley the title of honorary president and lifetime board member
  • 1959 - Smedley wrote The Story of Toastmasters: Reminiscences of the Founder
  • 1961 - Smedley wrote Beyond Basic Training
  • 1963 - Smedley wrote The Advanced Speaker
  • 1964 - Smedley's health began to decline
  • 1965 - Died in Santa Ana, California, United States

"We realize that the two most important factors in Toastmasters is Mentoring and Evaluations, there is no doubt that if these two are done well and there is a good Mentoring program, your club will be filled with spark plugs ready to fire upon request. Mentoring and evaluations create enthusiasm, and once you light that fire the only thing it needs is some kindling.."

Dr Ralph .C Smedley
